A struggle is approaching, sooner or later—a struggle much like that of the 
__19th century__, where we workers fought for the common good, where we 
secured __8-hour workdays__ and better wage conditions. And all of this is 
about to change.

Technology has changed the world; it has made our lives easier and allowed 
the middle class to prosper for a brief moment. However, that technology has 
increasingly been __usurped by Big Tech giants__ who seek to impoverish us.

We have handed the reins to large corporations that crave nothing but __power, money, and global influence__. They use our data to enrich 
themselves and, in doing so, distract us from the things that truly matter.

We are witnessing a world where __precariousness__ and the __automation of labor__ are becoming more evident every day.

This time, the world will bring __a different kind of poverty__; it will be 
unlike that of the 19th century. __The poverty of the 21st century__ will 
look like this: there will be food, there will be water, and a place to 
sleep—but there will be no rest, and time will become the most precious 
commodity.

If these giant companies achieve the long-awaited __AGI__, I dread to think 
what will happen to labor. It could lead to a world where corporations seize 
total power, returning us to a model of technological aristocracy and their 
vassals.

But it doesn't have to happen that way. History has taught us that the 
people, united, will never be defeated.

That is why the new struggle will not come from strikes in the streets; it 
will come digitally. Hacking, the disruption of information systems, and the 
media battle—that will be the new battlefield.

We must fight for change. It wasn't easy for the people of the 19th century, 
but they succeeded. Now it is our turn to fight for a better world and a 
better future. As José Mujica said:

"_Politics is the struggle for the happiness of everyone, but especially for those who have been left behind. Do not let them steal your hope, for hope is the engine of those who have nothing left but their strength to fight._"
